About Me

I'm Nate Harada, currently working with the research team at Fitbit. Before that I was a grad student at University of Michigan, and before that I did my undergrad at Tufts University. In grad school I worked in Zeeshan Syed's lab, investigating how deep learning can improve our understanding of heart diseases such as atrial fibrillation. My interests extend broadly to applying ML techniques to real world problems.

We recently published work at MICRO 2016 on applying deep sequence learning to static compiler optimization!
